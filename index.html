Who I Am

Gawx is a young artist and content creator based in Mexico. With over 2 Million Followers in his social media pages, Gawx inspires thousands of people to create. In July 2020, he released his first self designed clothing brand, which sold out in a few days. In December 2020 took place his first solo exhibition “Gawx In Paris” in Galerie Sakura Paris, France.
